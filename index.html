Shdati Website

We’re excited to introduce a specialized digital solution for teachers in both public and private sectors. Our platform enables educators to effortlessly generate professional PDF certificates for students by simply uploading an Excel file exported from the OpenEMIS system. We’ve recently enhanced the platform with powerful new features: Marks below the passing threshold are now automatically displayed in red and underlined, clearly indicating failing scores. Each student’s status is visually represented with an "X" — showing whether the student passed, has to repeat the year, or is required to make up a subject, based on their final marks. This tool is designed to save time and effort while delivering clean, print-ready certificates — making the end-of-term process easier, faster, and more efficient.
